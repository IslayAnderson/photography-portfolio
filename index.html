What do I shoot with?

I currently use very little gear, after years of letting gear and tech specs get in the way of developing as a photographer and finding my voice. My main shooter is a Pentax ME Super with a 28-85mm zoom and a variable ND filter. I also have a 105mm unbranded prime that I take with me on hikes, One of those Lomography reloadable “disposable” cameras I take with me to family events and with friends and if I remember to take batteries with me my Nikon Coolpix l20 bridge camera however, I want to replace this with a DSLR.
